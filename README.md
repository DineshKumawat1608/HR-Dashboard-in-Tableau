# 👥 HR Analytics Tableau Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to help HR professionals analyze workforce data, monitor employee demographics, evaluate salary distribution, and explore organizational insights through an intuitive reporting interface.

The dashboard combines executive-level HR metrics with employee-level reporting to support data-driven workforce planning and decision-making.

---

# 📌 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ HR Overview

Provides a high-level overview of workforce performance and organizational metrics.

### Highlights

- Active Employees
- Hired Employees
- Terminated Employees
- Department Distribution
- Employee Locations
- Gender Distribution
- Education Analysis
- Education vs Performance
- Salary Analysis
- Age vs Salary Analysis

---

## 2️⃣ Employee Details

Provides detailed employee-level reporting with interactive filtering.

### Highlights

- Employee Information
- Demographics
- Department & Job Role
- Geographic Information
- Salary Details
- Employment Status
- Length of Employment
- Dynamic Search & Filters

# 📊 Business Questions Answered

- What is the current active workforce?
- Which departments have the largest workforce?
- How are employees distributed across locations?
- What is the gender distribution?
- How does education relate to employee performance?
- What is the salary distribution across age groups?
- Which employees belong to specific departments or locations?
- How long have employees been with the organization?
